About Me

Mr. Shubhankar Dubey was born in Rewa , madhya pradesh ,India. His father Mr. Arun Kumar Dubey (M.sc,LLB) is his ideal. His mother Mrs. Anupma Dubey. Shubhankar Dubey did his middle and primary studies from Saraswati Shishu Mandir, Mauganj with 90% marks. He completed his High Schooling from Saraswati shishu mandir Mauganj with 81.67% marks. Then he was admitted at Excell. Boys Govt. School Mauganj, Rewa. Mr. Dubey passed his Higher Secondary exam having 73% marks with Mathematics. After that he was admitted at Govt. polytechnic collage, Shahdol (M.P.) for (Mining and Mine surveying ). Then after one year he Awarded Hungaricum Stipendium Scholarship by Tempus Public Foundation, University of Debrecen, Hungary for (Computer Science Engineering, BSc ). and he Started his study in the faculty of informatics,university of debrecen,hungary.
